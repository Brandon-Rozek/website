Inspired from this [recipe](https://www.simplyrecipes.com/recipes/pressure_cooker_paella_with_chicken_and_sausage/).

This dish is traditionally made with white rice and it
is tastier that way, but I've been experimenting with eating
more brown rice recently.

# Ingridients

- 1.5 cups of brown/white rice
- 2.5 cups of chicken broth if brown rice used otherwise 1.5 cups
- 1 bag of frozen peas
- 2 shallots
- Red bell pepper
- Chorizo
- Half tablespoon tumeric
- 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
- 1 teaspoon salt 


# Recipe

1. Dice up the bell pepper and shallots, and throw onto a pan on the stove.
2. Take the chorizo and slice it up.
1. Saute pepper, shallots, and chorizo on the pan for 5 minutes.
2. Throw everything into the instant pot.
3. Set the pressure cook to high and 25 minutes
4. Let it naturally release for 10 minutes

Enjoy!
