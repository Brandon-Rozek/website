Prep Time: 5 Minutes

Cook Time: 40 Minutes

Total Time: 45 minutes

## Ingredients

- Yellow Rice
- 2 tablespoons Tzatziki Sauce
- Lamb
- Olive Oil
- 1/4 Cup finely diced onion
- 2 tablespoons garlic powder
- 1 teaspoon paprika
- 1/2 teaspoon ginger
