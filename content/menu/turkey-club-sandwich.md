Prep Time: 0 minutes
Cook Time: 7 minutes

## Ingredients
- Tortilla
- Shredded Mexican Cheese
- Turkey
- Chipotle Mayo
- Bacon (Optional)

## Recipe

1. Warm up tortillas on the skillet.
2. Add cheese, wait until it melts
3. Once melted, add chipotle mayo
4. Then add 1-2 slices of turkey
5. Fold in half and wait 2 minutes per side
