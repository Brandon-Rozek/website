Creates 4 cups of fruit smoothie.

I tried this with bannanas and mangos.

## Ingredients
- 300g of whichever fruit you want
- 6 spoons of yogurt
- 1.5 cups milk

## Recipe

Throw all into a blender and blend until smooth
